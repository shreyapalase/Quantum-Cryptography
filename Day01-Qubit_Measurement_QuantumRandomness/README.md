# Qubit,Measurement and Quantum Randomness

This folder contains self-generated study material designed to build a **clear and practical understanding of Quantum Cryptography**, 
starting from fundamental concepts and moving toward applied intuition.

---

## What You Will Learn

By going through the contents of this folder, you will learn:

- The basics of quantum information and qubits  
- How quantum measurement and wavefunction collapse work  
- The importance of true randomness in cryptography  
- Why quantum randomness is superior to classical pseudo-randomness  
- How simple quantum circuits generate random bits  
- The conceptual foundation behind quantum cryptographic protocols  

---

## Folder Contents

 **1. `Day01_Qubit_and_Measurement.ipynb`**
- Interactive notebook
- Demonstrates a simple quantum circuit
- Uses a single qubit, Hadamard gate, and measurement
- Shows results using different numbers of shots
- Helps visualize quantum randomness experimentally

 2. **`Day01_notes.md`**
- Well-structured theoretical notes
- Explains core concepts of quantum cryptography
- Covers qubits, measurement collapse, randomness, and cryptographic relevance
- Suitable for revision and conceptual clarity
